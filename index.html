Bubble Letters

Instructions

  • Choose one of the levels, easy, medium or hard
  • Write down what you see on the left side,
    by clicking on the letters on the right side.
  • To undo, click on the added later and it will disappear.
  • To remove all of the letters at once, click on the X.
  • When you are done, click on OK!
